About Chetak Group

Mr.Bhaskarbhai Desai is the man behind the hugely successful brand "CHETAK" in the area of Kheda district.

In 1970,Mr.Desai started a small-scale enterprise called Chetak Moters. The core strength of Mr.Bhaskerbhai Desai with their two sons Mr.Atul Desai & Mr.Sanjay Desai, who have notched up one success after another.

In 1976 Both brothers had founded Chetak Sports Club with other dedicated friends & persons to serve the society with unique way. The Club had organised Night Cricket matches & Chetak Navarati festivals which is still having long lasting impression on garba lovers.

In 1981, They established Chetak petrol pump at the heart of Nadiad City, which is still the flagship company of the Chetak Group. The legacy continued with another petrol pump at Sandhana in 1984.

The Grand Chetak project was launched in 2016 by Desai Brothers with their sons who possess royalty and elegance desire for providing a perfect wedding destination.
